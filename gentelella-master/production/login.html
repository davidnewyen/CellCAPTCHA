What is our project ?

Welcome to cellCAPTCHA, a tool that has the potential to bridge the realms of academia, industry, and the general public.

Your contribution can help the science!

In addition of teaching you about scientific research, and keeping you awake for hours, our stem cell classification game aims to improve the accuracy of the neural net prediction. You will be challenged to guess the maturation stages of stem cell differentiation with multiple game modes, tracking your performance breakdown over time.
